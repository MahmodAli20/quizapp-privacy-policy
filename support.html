❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account. This action is permanent.

How does scoring work?

Win = you earn correct answers as points. Draw = both get +1 point. Lose = no points added.

Why can't I find my friend?

Make sure you entered their exact username. Usernames are case-sensitive.

What happens if I lose connection?

If your connection drops during a battle, you may lose progress. A stable internet connection is recommended.

How many questions per battle?

Each battle has 10 questions with 15 seconds per question.
