# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 22, 2026

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Username
- Email address
- Password (encrypted)

### Game Data
- Scores
- Battle history
- Game statistics
- Wins/Losses record

### Profile Information
- Avatar selection
- Account creation date

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- Create and manage your account
- Track game scores and leaderboards
- Enable real-time battles with other users
- Improve app functionality and user experience
- Provide customer support when needed

## Data Storage

Your data is securely stored using:
- **Firebase Authentication**: For secure login and account management
- **Cloud Firestore**: For game data, scores, and user profiles
- All data is encrypted in transit and at rest

## Data Sharing

We value your privacy:
- ✅ Username and score appear on public leaderboard
- ❌ We do not sell your personal data
- ❌ We do not share data with third parties
- ❌ We do not use your data for advertising

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- View all your data in the Settings screen
- Update your username and avatar anytime
- Request account deletion by contacting us
- Access your game history and statistics

## Children's Privacy

- Users must be at least 13 years old to register
- We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13
- If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures:
- Firebase's built-in security rules
- Encrypted data transmission
- Regular security updates

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be:
- Posted in this document with updated date
- Notified to users via in-app notification for significant changes

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:
- Email: **your-email@example.com**
- Through the app's Settings screen
